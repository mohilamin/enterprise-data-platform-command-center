# Metrics

- Data product health score: average mapped signal value by data product.
- Platform health score: average data product health.
- SLA compliance rate: SLA signals marked healthy or watch divided by all SLA signals.
- AI readiness score: average AI, RAG, MLOps, and model-related signals by product.
- Governance risk score: average governance, policy, trust, conflict, and least-privilege signals by product.
