# STAR Story

## Situation
Enterprise data and AI health signals are fragmented across tools and teams.

## Task
Build a unified command center for platform health, governance, reliability, AI readiness, and downstream impact.

## Action
Created synthetic signals, normalization, scoring, incident correlation, lineage graphs, scorecards, API, dashboard, tests, Docker, and CI.

## Result
Delivered a reproducible portfolio project demonstrating platform-level data engineering.
